Codon specific distribution of reads

To examine the global landscape of 80S ribosomes, we average the position-specific RPF densities of individual genes into a composite metagene, in which each gene was first normalized for its overall density of RPFs and then weighted equally in the average. The size of the initiation ramps was shown to vary between studies and has been attributed to use of CHX pre-treatment. Here we compare the data with an average of previous studies, using either CHX or Flash Freeze pre-treatment. Standard deviation represented by area shading around the lines.
